Velabahleke High School

Welcome to Velabahleke, where every child matters, we are about transforming lives. Velabahleke is also about addressing the imbalances of the past which were caused by the apartheid albatross. The school offers a diverse curriculum and prides itself with the dedicated educators who do not only offer quality education to our learners but also go beyond their call of duty with extra-curricular activities which seeks to instil leadership skills, values and morals to our learners.

Alumni

The Velabahleke High School Alumni is a body of former learners that seek to make a contribution back into the school that has made them who they have become in society and in the corporate world. We make contribution in various ways according to our generousity and capability. The Alumni is currently involved in the following programs: Assisting Grade 12 with their university applications, Sponsoring of Uniforms to those who are coming from needy families Participating in the fomration of the Velabahleke High School Foundation. The current chairperson of the Governing body is also a member of the schools alumni.
